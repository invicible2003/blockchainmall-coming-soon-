Features of BlockchainMall.org

$ZD-Powered Ecosystem

ZarrazDollar ($ZD) is the native utility token, powering purchases, Boost Tokens, affiliate rewards, and premium tools. Enables seamless, incentive-driven participation across the platform.

Merchant Visibility Boosting via Boost Tokens

Merchants use $ZD to buy Boost Tokens, which increase their product or store visibility. More Boost Tokens = higher placement in search and featured listings. Boosted content carries a “Promoted” tag, enhancing credibility and reach.

Mall AI Assistant for Shoppers & Merchants

Mall AI Assistant delivers a smarter, faster shopping experience with curated recommendations and real-time help. For merchants, it offers deep analytics, pricing intelligence, and automated business optimizations.

BlockchainMall.org Cashback System

Shoppers earn USDT cashback on eligible purchases. Cashback rates are dynamic and exclusive to the platform. Premium Shoppers in BlockchainMall.org Plus enjoy boosted rewards and early access to cashback events.

Complete KYC & Business Verification

All merchants undergo rigorous verification to ensure trust, authenticity, and compliance. Verified merchants gain priority placement and shopper trust badges.

Abandoned Cart Recovery Engine

AI tracks abandoned carts and sends smart, personalized follow-ups. Merchants can incentivize recovery using $ZD-powered discounts or offers.

Affiliate Program Dashboard

Built-in affiliate tracking for shoppers and merchants to earn USDT rewards for referrals. All tracked transparently on-chain. Premium Merchants gain advanced tools for campaign customization and reporting.

Custom Storefront Editor

Each merchant controls their store’s design using drag-and-drop tools or code editing. Enables powerful brand expression within the BlockchainMall ecosystem.

Secure, Transparent, On-Chain Payments

Every transaction is securely recorded on the blockchain, ensuring zero fraud or chargebacks. All payments and histories are fully verifiable and tamper-proof.

Smart Search & Discovery Engine

AI ranks products based on relevance, shopper behavior, and quality—not just advertising spend. Helps users discover what they truly want while giving all merchants a fair chance.

Merchant Dashboard with 37 Advanced Features

  • Competitive Intelligence Panel
  • Product Heatmaps
  • AI Content Generator
  • Wallet Automation
  • Reward Campaign Tools
  • Real-Time Chat with Customers
  • Tax & Global Shipping Assistant
  • and more tailored for serious e-commerce growth.

24/7 Global Support System

Shoppers and merchants get round-the-clock assistance through live chat, AI, and email channels. Critical issues are escalated to human teams for swift resolution, ensuring trust and reliability.

Global Shipping at Fair Rates + Free Shipping Promotions

Affordable international shipping negotiated through strategic logistics partners. Regular free shipping campaigns on select products, brands, or during special promotions. Merchants can also offer $ZD-funded shipping discounts to increase sales.
